Product Information

Monster Energy

$2.50 - $3.50

Monster Energy comes in a 16 fl oz (473 ml) can with about 160 mg of caffeine per serving. It contains roughly 54 g of sugar and about 210 calories. Key ingredients include taurine (1000 mg), ginseng extract, L-carnitine, and B vitamins (B3, B6, B12) to support energy production and alertness.

Red Bull

$2.50 - $3.00

Red Bull is sold in a 8.4 fl oz (250 ml) can containing about 80 mg of caffeine and 27 g of sugar (110 calories). It includes taurine (1000 mg), glucuronolactone, and B-complex vitamins designed to improve focus and reduce fatigue.

C4 Energy

$2.50 - $4.00

C4 Energy is a 16 fl oz drink with about 200 mg of caffeine and 0–5 calories (sugar-free versions use sucralose). It contains CarnoSyn® beta-alanine (1600 mg), beta-power, and vitamin B12 (250% DV) to support workout performance and energy endurance.

Ghost Energy

$3.00 - $4.50

Ghost Energy comes in a 16 fl oz can with 200 mg caffeine, zero sugar, and about 5–15 calories. It includes nootropics like Alpha-GPC (150 mg), NeuroFactor® (100 mg coffee fruit extract), and taurine (1000 mg) for focus and energy.

Reign Total Body Fuel

$2.50 - $3.50

Reign is a 16 fl oz energy drink with 300 mg of caffeine, zero sugar, and about 10 calories. It contains BCAAs (L-leucine, isoleucine, valine at 1000 mg each), electrolytes, and CoQ10 for hydration and performance support.

Bang Energy

$2.50 - $3.50

Bang Energy comes in a 16 fl oz can with 300 mg caffeine, 0 sugar, and 0 calories. It includes Super Creatine (dipeptide bonded creatine), BCAAs (5,000 mg), and electrolytes designed for performance and mental focus.

Rockstar Energy

$2.00 - $3.50

Rockstar Energy typically comes in a 16 fl oz can with 160 mg of caffeine, 31-63g of sugar depending on the variant, and around 120-250 calories. It contains taurine, guarana extract, ginseng, and added B vitamins for sustained energy release.

5-hour Energy

$2.00 - $3.00

5-hour Energy is a 2 fl oz shot containing about 200 mg caffeine, 0 sugar, and only 4 calories. It includes B vitamins (B6, B12 at over 8000% DV), amino acids like taurine and phenylalanine, and is designed for quick absorption and short-term energy boost.
